TakeOver'26 Hackathon

Anchor — Business Validation Portfolio

AI-Native Business Builder Framework applied to modern dining operations at Indian Accent, New Delhi.

1
Understand
Business Model
Inventive modern Indian fine-dining experiences. Revenue is driven by high-value covers (tasting menus at ₹7,500/person, average checks at ₹10,000 for 2-3 guests).
Customers
HNIs, corporate groups, tourists, and families celebrating special milestones. Bookings come through web, email, and phone lines.
Workflow
Customer sends reservation query → Hostess manually checks book → Captures details → Visit occurs → Table bill settled → No formal follow-up.
Constraints
Staff limits during rush hours; manual booking errors (dietary/names); 11:00 PM - 12:00 PM offline window where queries remain unanswered.
2
Watch
  • People: Hostess spends 2.5 hours daily answering calls, checking table logs, and resolving duplicate entries in Microsoft Excel.
  • Process: Incoming reservation calls placed on hold (avg. 4 mins queue time) during peak dinner rush, causing frequent call abandonment.
  • Information: Crucial client details (diabetic, Jain, peanut-allergies) stored on scattered paper slips and WhatsApp threads rather than a central index.
  • Communication: Average of 12 missed calls/day logged during night hours and peak service windows when lines were busy.
  • Loss/Waste: Primary tables left empty due to no-shows (2 tables/week) because the hostess lacked time to make confirmation follow-up calls.
3
Ask
Daily Enquiries
~45 reservation enquiries per day.
Staff Time Consumption
Manual reservation log entries and callback confirmations consume 15-20 hours per week of the hostess's time.
Average Transaction Value
₹10,000 per table booking, with an average gross margin of 70%.
Leakage Rate
15% of weekend bookings fail to convert due to response delays exceeding 3 hours.
Repeated Errors
Kitchen friction caused by transcription errors on dietary requests (e.g., serving gluten-heavy dishes to celiac guests).

6
Define the Problem
Indian Accent is losing approximately ₹3,17,000 per month because reservation enquiries are handled manually via phone/email, leading to missed calls during rushes, unconfirmed table no-shows, and delayed follow-ups. This happens daily and directly affects hostess productivity, kitchen preparation efficiency, and bottom-line profit margins.

8
Select One MVP

We chose to build an AI-Native Workflow (Anchor) to solve Problem A:

  • RAG Chat Engine: Dynamically answers restaurant-specific menu and operational questions with 0% hallucinations.
  • AI structured extraction: Automatically extracts customer name and phone details from raw chat sentences to qualify leads.
  • Owner Dashboard & Copilot: Automates lead analysis (revenue potential, priority, urgency) and gives owners conversational metrics insights.
