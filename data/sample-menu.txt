INDIAN ACCENT — Restaurant Information & Menu Guide
====================================================

ABOUT US
--------
Indian Accent showcases inventive Indian cuisine by complementing the flavours and traditions of India with global ingredients and techniques. Located at The Lodhi, Lodhi Road, New Delhi, 110003, Indian Accent has been on Asia's 50 Best Restaurants list for over 10 years, currently ranked #26. It has been recognized by Time Magazine amongst the 'World's 100 Greatest Places' and voted the No. 1 Restaurant in India by Condé Nast Traveller.

Our Executive Chef, Shantanu Mehrotra, has been with Indian Accent since it opened at The Manor in 2009 and has created innumerable award-winning menus over the last 15 years. He works with EHV International as Executive Chef at Indian Accent New Delhi and New York.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
---------------------------

Q: Do you have outdoor seating?
A: We have a beautiful terrace area available during the winter months (October through March). Please request terrace seating when making your reservation.

Q: Is there a kids' menu?
A: While we don't have a dedicated kids' menu, our chefs are happy to prepare simpler dishes for younger guests. Please let us know when booking.

Q: Can I bring my own wine/cake?
A: Corkage is ₹3,000 per bottle (750ml). Outside cakes are welcome with a plating charge of ₹500.

Q: Do you offer home delivery?
A: We do not offer home delivery for our main restaurant menu. However, select desserts and gift boxes are available for pickup through our online store.

Q: Is the restaurant wheelchair accessible?
A: Yes, Indian Accent is fully wheelchair accessible with ramp access and an accessible restroom.

Q: What is the average cost for two?
A: A la carte dining for two (without alcohol) typically ranges from ₹6,000 – ₹10,000 plus taxes. The tasting menu for two would be ₹15,000 plus taxes.

Q: Do you have Wi-Fi?
A: Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi is available for all guests. Ask your server for the password.

Q: Can I host a surprise birthday/anniversary dinner?
A: Absolutely! We love hosting special occasions. Please email us the details and we'll arrange a cake, special decoration, and even a personalized menu card. No extra charge for standard decoration; premium decoration packages start at ₹5,000.
